
## 2026-06-20 — Initial public release

**Scope:** Full list (3–6 letter words)

**Rationale:** The source list comes from Wordnik's open-source wordlist, which functions as a dictionary. As such, it includes all attested English words regardless of suitability as puzzle answers. Prior to public release, an unpublished curation pass removed words deemed inappropriate as answers, including offensive terms, obscure abbreviations, and other poor puzzle candidates. Specific removals from this phase were not individually logged.

**Counts at release:**
- 3-letter: 1,341 words
- 4-letter: 5,564 words
- 5-letter: 11,784 words
- 6-letter: 18,169 words
